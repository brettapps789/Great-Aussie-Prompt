2.  **Model Tuning & Distillation:** Using Vertex AI, we can take global models like Gemini 1.5 Pro and 'distill' them using localized Australian datasets. This creates a model that retains global reasoning power while gaining a deep, native understanding of the Australian market.
3.  **Private Endpoints:** We implement Private Service Connect to ensure that our AI workforce communicates over a private network, completely isolated from the public internet.
